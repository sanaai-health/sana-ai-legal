Common Issues

Speech Recognition Not Working

  • Ensure microphone permissions are granted in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone
  • Check speech recognition permissions in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Speech Recognition
  • Try restarting the app if voice input becomes unresponsive

Subscription Issues

  • Subscriptions are managed through the App Store
  • Contact Apple Support for billing inquiries
  • For account-specific subscription questions, email us at help.sanaai@gmail.com

Data Privacy

  • All health data is stored locally on your device
  • Your conversations with Sage are processed securely
  • We do not sell or share your personal health information
